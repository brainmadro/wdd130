Featured Trips

The Magdalena River Adventure

Embark on a thrilling 2-hour journey down the Magdalena River, Colombia's principal river. Navigate through the gentle rapids suitable for beginners and families, all while surrounded by the lush rainforest and the chance to spot exotic wildlife.

The Cauca River Expedition

The Cauca River offers a more challenging 3-hour white water rafting experience. With its Class III and IV rapids, it's perfect for those seeking an adrenaline rush.

The Amazon River Odyssey

For the ultimate adventure, the Amazon River all-day trip is unmatched. This expedition will take you deep into the heart of the Amazon Rainforest.
